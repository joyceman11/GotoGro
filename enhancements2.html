Image that cycles over:

This allows the company to let customers know that the company delivers fast and also ships worldwide. This is a fun way to capture the users attention. When the customer clicks on the enquire page, this image cycle will start automatically. It will have a cycle interval of 3 seconds so it gives the user ample time to look at the advertisment.
