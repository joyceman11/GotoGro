Things about me!

Growing up in the crazy quick paced city of Melbourne has it's uniqueness, ranging from weather, sports to it's diverse culture. These binding elements make this city incredibly different from others, as Melbourne has many aspects that other cities don't have. One of the things I particularly love is being able to go to the O'brien Ice House. During the year when the AHIL(Australian Ice Hockey League) is on I love to spend hours watching each match, alongside my friends. When Melbourne Ice or Melbourne Mustangs have a home game at the O'brien Ice House, you'll often find a couple of my friends and I at one of the games. Despite it's popularity with some melburnians, this sport is quite underrated in Australia, in comparison to it's popularity in America and Canada. I hope in the future many more people will grow to love this sport, as it is filled with lots of fun and action! Not only do I love ice hockey, I also love spending time with my friends by going out for lunch or inviting friends over to do some photography.
